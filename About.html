Welcome To Dron-AI

Dron-Al is on a mission to build India's Al talent pipeline, shaping the next generation of innovators and positioning our nation as a Vishwaguru in technology. A CSR initiative by Code Karo Yaaro and Suvidha Foundation, Dron-Al offers a free, one-year, job-oriented Diploma in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning designed to make every learner industry-ready.

Through hands-on learning, live projects, and research-driven exploration, learners gain the skills and experience needed to thrive in the global Al ecosystem from Machine Learning to Natural Language Processing and beyond. Powered by Suvidha Foundation's legacy of 1,00,000+ internships and 80,000+ successful placements, Dron-Al is not just a training program - it's a launchpad for your Al career, your innovation journey, and your contribution to India's technological growth story.

About Suvidha Foundation

Suvidha Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering youth through technology education. Our DronAI program represents the future of artificial intelligence and deep learning education, designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

We believe in creating opportunities for every individual to excel in the rapidly evolving tech landscape. Through our comprehensive programs, we've successfully trained thousands of students and professionals, helping them secure positions in top-tier companies worldwide.
